Fiverr works

Twilio Design

A design I made in WPF (with MaterialDesign) for adding and managing Twilio accounts and Cards. Each account has its own set of cards, and all are saved to a database. Cards are programatically added to Queue or Selected based on their queue status. I also implemented a simple MVVM pattern for taking care of the operations, such as selecting accounts and updating layout.

View Design

Zodiac Sign Bot

A Twitch.tv bot for interacting with viewers on a new music-related channel. The bot automatically connects to the channel and whispers user a question: "What is your zodiac sign?". When the user responds, the bot sends out a random fun-fact based on the sign picked.

Arduino Twitch Bot

An application designed in WPF that acts as a Twitch.tv bot. The bot listens to Channel Points redemptions in the specified channel. Whenever a reward is redeemed, the bot sends out a signal (int) to Arduino using SerialPort connection. The channel name the bot connects to, as well as the signal for each reward, is customizable inside the app.

Twitch Copypasta Bot

A Twitch.tv bot that connects to a channel and automatically saves "copypastas" (messages that are spammed a lot in the chatroom). The application has a graphical interface created in WPF (using Material Design for XAML), and a database integration using Entity Framework. Copypastas can also be added manually; they can be browsed, marked as favourite, etc.

Mobile Phone

A project made with .NET Core 3.1, WPF and Material Design. It offers an interface looking just like a modern mobile phone, and includes several other working applications, such as: Calculator, Notepad, Timers, Phonebook and Photo Viewer. Implemented the MVVM architecture. Some data (notes, phone contacts) is saved to a local database using Entity Framework Core.
